curtails NSA surveillance abuses.</li> <li><span>The FISA Improvements Act</span> legalizes bulk data collection of phone records.</li> </ul> <p>We need to tell Congress to pass the USA Freedom Act freedomact"> <h4>USA Freedom Act</h4> <p>The USA Freedom Act (<a href="https://scout.sunlightfoundation.com/item/bill/s1599-113/uniting-and-strengthening-america-by-fulfilling-rights-and-ending-eavesdropping-dragnet-collection-and-online-monitoring-act">S. 1599</a>) is a bipartisan bill introd-uced by Sen. Patrick Leahy (D-VT) and Rep. Jim Sensenbrenner (R-WI). </p> <p>If passed, this bill would be a <strong>substantial improvement</strong> to America’s laws regarding mass surveillance.</p> <ul> <li>It brings <strong>new levels of transparency</strong> to the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Foreign_Intelligence_Surveillance_Court">Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Court</a> (FISA court)</li> <li>It introduces <strong>a special advocate to champion civil liberties</strong> in the FISA court</li> <li>It appears to create <strong>new statutory limits on mass surveillance</strong> by the NSA.</li> </ul> <p>However, <strong>this bill is a floor, not a ceiling</strong>. Here's how it could be improved:</p> <ul> <li>It could clarify in plain language that bulk surveillance is illegal and prevent the FBI from issuing National Security Letters without prior review by a judge.</li> <li>Include language to stop the NSA from undermining international encryption standards.</li> <li>Have stronger language to protect the privacy of people outside of the United States.</li> </ul> <p>The USA FREEDOM Act is one step in the right direction, with many more steps that need to follow. </p> <p><strong>Read more</strong>:</p> <ul> <li><a href="https://www.eff.org/deeplinks/2013/11/floor-not-ceiling-supporting-usa-freedom-act-step-towards-less-surveillance">EFF’s analysis of the USA FREEDOM Act</a></li> <li><a href="https://www.aclu.org/blog/national-security/usa-freedom-act-real-spying-reform">ACLU’s blog post about the USA FREEDOM Act</a></li> </ul> </div> <div class="col-sm-6 lawbox fisaact"> <h4>FISA Improvements Act</h4> <p>The FISA Improvements Act, is a bill written by the intelligence community and Sen. Dianne Feinstein (D-CA).</p> <p>If the FISA Improvements Act (S. 1631) were to pass:</p> <ul> <li>It <strong>would codify the controversial bulk telephone records surveillance program</strong> of the NSA.</li> <li>It would <strong>allow the NSA to continue to collect telephone records</strong> of hundreds of millions of Americans not suspected of any crime. </li> <li>It would permit the NSA to <strong>restart the bulk collection of Internet communication records</strong>—an extremely invasive, secret program the government attempted under dubious legal ground but abandoned because it wasn't effective.</li> </ul> <p><strong>Read more</strong>:</p> <ul> <li><a href="https://www.eff.org/deeplinks/2013/12/54-civil-liberties-and-public-interest-organizations-oppose-fisa-improvements-act">54 civil liberties and public interest groups opposing the FISA Improvements Act</li> <li><a href="https://www.aclu.org/blog/national-security-technology-and-liberty/sen-dianne-feinsteins-nsa-reforms-bad-privacy-bad">The ACLU’s blog post on the FISA Improvements Act</a></li> <li><a href="https://action.eff.org/o/9042/p/dia/action3/common/public/?action_KEY=9437">EFF’s alert about the FISA Improvements Act</a></li> </ul> </div> </div> </div> </div> </div> </div> <a name="banner-live"></a> <div class="container banner-live" id="actions"> <div class="row"> <div class="col-md-10 col-lg-8 centered"> <div class="step row"> <h4 style="margin-bottom:15px">UPDATE: Add the Banner to Your Site</h4> <p class="text-center" style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:15px;">First time here?
